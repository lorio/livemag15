His echo,
stone gray and flanked
by trees fastened to the ground,
cemented in their small brown squares,
wild roots below,
hidden,
an undiscovered text.
He waits.

Her answer, plaintive,
soft and foreign,
not the one he hoped,
the jeweled clarion,
others,
but not the one,
the lyric withered,
music weakened,
went.
